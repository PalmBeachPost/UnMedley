Meddling with Medley

Welcome to UnMedley - the cure to all your Medley woes. Or at least a few of them.

This is a framework built on top of Medley to help you quickly create custom presentations for your stories. UnMedley, at its heart, is a CSS/JS framework. But you don't have to know CSS or javascript to churn out stories in custom layouts.

All you need are some HTML chops and/or crazy copy-paste skills.

Please note that this is a work in progress. You can add new templates or use existing ones. You can contribute to the template library or pretend this is just magic.

You can help me write help documents or just yell at me when it doesn't work. Use it as you please.

Steps to greatness

Just follow these instructions to make your own awesome story presentation.

  • Pick a template from the list below. (More templates coming soon)
  • Follow instructions on the template page to download a file and update it with your story.
  • Create a flatpage in Medley with the "Return to site header and footer" option selected. (This may vary for different templates.)
  • Copy everything between the HTML body tags into the flatpage content section.
  • Save and test on all devices.
  • Brag about your story on social media.
